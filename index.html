Construction Engineer & Engineering Educator

I am a dedicated Construction Engineer and Lecturer with over 7 years of comprehensive experience spanning both the construction industry and engineering education. My unique dual expertise allows me to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical construction application, delivering exceptional value to every project and every student I mentor.

My professional philosophy centers on the integration of modern construction technologies — including BIM, advanced structural analysis, and digital project management tools — with proven engineering practices. I believe that sustainable development in construction begins with well-trained professionals who understand both the science and the craft of building.

As a construction lecturer, I am committed to developing the next generation of engineers by connecting classroom learning with real-world construction challenges. My industry connections and active project involvement ensure that my students receive education that is current, relevant, and practically applicable.
